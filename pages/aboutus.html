How It Began

The co-founder had a passion for wood work and would spend his time with he's father building furniture for their household or for family and friends that were not well off and could not afford furniture's from stores.

Many years later after he graduated and started creating furniture in his father's garage.people in the neighbourhood saw his designs and started purchasing one item after the next.

He moved his small business from his home town in 1956 to a city where he opened up his business, which today is known as F-studio and is still going strong.

What We stand for!

our goal is to make affordable modern furniture's available for our consumers all over the globe and open up stores globally.

The team we have believes in working with the consumer to find their product that goes with their home environment. We strive to build a good and trusting relationship, with each other and our customers, as we believe that our respect costs nothing and word of mouth is more powerful than an article online.
